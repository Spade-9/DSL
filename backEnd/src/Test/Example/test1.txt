Step welcome
    Speak $name + "您好，请问有什么可以帮您?"
    Listen 10 # Listen for 10 seconds
    Branch "投诉" complainProc
    Branch "账单" billProc
    Silence silenceProc
    Default defaultProc
Step complainProc
    Speak "您的意见是我们改进工作的动力，请问您还有什么补充?"
    Listen 10 # Listen for 10 seconds
    Default thanks
Step thanks
    Speak "感谢您的来电，再见"
    Exit
Step billProc
    Speak "您的本月账单是" + $amount + "元，感谢您的来电，再见"
    Exit
Step silenceProc
    Speak "听不清，请您大声一点可以吗"
    Listen 10 # Listen for 10 seconds
    Branch "投诉" complainProc
    Branch "账单" billProc
    Silence silenceProc
    Default defaultProc
Step defaultProc
    Speak "抱歉，我没有听清。您可以说：投诉 或 账单。"
    Exit
